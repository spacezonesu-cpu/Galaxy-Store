Shipping FAQs

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-8 business days within Kenya. Express shipping takes 3-5 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but typically range from 14-25 business days.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes, we offer free standard shipping on all orders over $90 within the Kenya. International orders qualify for free shipping at higher thresholds that vary by destination.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our interface to monitor your package's journey.
