Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within Kenya. Express shipping options are available for delivery within 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by destination but generally take 7-14 business days.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition with packaging. Electronics may have specific return conditions. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Proof of purchase is required for all returns.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your Galaxy Store account and visiting the 'Order History' section.

Do you offer technical support for products?

Yes, we offer technical support for all products purchased from Galaxy Store. Our support team is available via phone, email, and live chat during business hours. Many products also include manufacturer warranties.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including M-Pesa, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels.
