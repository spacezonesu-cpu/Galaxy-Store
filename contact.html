Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-8 business days within major counties in Kenya. Express shipping is available for an additional fee with delivery in 3-6 business days. International shipping typically takes 14-21 business days depending on the destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unused products in their original packaging. Returns are free for defective items. For change-of-mind returns, a restocking fee may apply. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.

Do you offer technical support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive technical support for all our products. Our support team is available Monday-Friday from 9AM to 6PM via phone, email, or live chat. We also have an extensive knowledge base with tutorials and troubleshooting guides.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our interface to monitor your package's journey.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 4 counties nationwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Some restrictions may apply for certain products due to export regulations or compatibility issues.
