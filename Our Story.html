Our Cosmic Journey

From a small idea to a universe of innovation - this is the story of Galaxy Store

How It All Began

Galaxy Store was founded in 2015 by tech enthusiasts who believed that cutting-edge technology should be accessible to everyone. What started as a small online store operating from a garage has now grown into a leading destination for tech enthusiasts across the galaxy.

Our founders, Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen, shared a vision of creating a place where people could discover the latest innovations in technology while receiving expert advice and exceptional customer service.

Our Growth Journey

By 2018, Galaxy Store had expanded to include a physical location in the heart of Nairobi's tech district. Our team grew from just 2 founders to 15 dedicated employees, each sharing the same passion for technology and customer satisfaction.

We carefully curated our product selection to include only the most innovative and reliable tech products, from cutting-edge smartphones to smart home devices that transform how people interact with their living spaces.

Our Mission

At Galaxy Store, our mission is to bring the universe of innovative technology within everyone's reach. We believe that technology should empower, inspire, and connect people across the globe.

We're committed to providing exceptional products, expert guidance, and stellar customer service that's truly out of this world.
