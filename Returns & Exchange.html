Our Return Policy

At Galaxy Store, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help with our straightforward return and exchange process.

30-Day Return Window

Return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Easy Returns

Start your return online and print a prepaid shipping label in just a few clicks.

Free Exchanges

Exchange any item for a different size or color at no additional cost.

Full Refunds

Receive a full refund to your original payment method once we process your return.

How to Return an Item

1

Start Your Return

Contact us through our interface and ask for good-return services to initiate your return.

2

Pack Your Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all accessories and tags attached.

3

Print Label

Print the prepaid shipping label we provide and attach it to your package.

4

Ship Your Return

Drop your package at any UPS location or schedule a pickup.

Policy Details

Eligible Items

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery
  • Products must be unused and in original condition
  • All original packaging and accessories must be included
  • Tags must still be attached to apparel items
  • Software and digital products are non-returnable

Non-Returnable Items

  • Software, digital downloads, and subscription services
  • Personalized or custom-made products
  • Opened software and video games
  • Gift cards and downloadable software
  • Products missing serial numbers or UPCs
